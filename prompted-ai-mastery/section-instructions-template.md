## Section-Specific Instructions

### 1. Assessment (AI Learning Journey Self-Reflection)

**WHO:** Individual employees at any level  
**PURPOSE:** Identify your current AI literacy stage and growth opportunities  
**TIME:** 5-10 minutes  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Rate each statement honestly (1=Rarely, 5=Consistently)
2. Click "Discover Your Learning Stage" to see your profile
3. Use results to identify which areas need development
4. Revisit monthly to track progress

---

### 2. ROI Calculator (Business Impact Calculator)

**WHO:** Managers, L&D leaders, executives making training investment decisions  
**PURPOSE:** Quantify the financial return of AI literacy training for your organization  
**TIME:** 2-3 minutes  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Enter number of employees who will receive training
2. Input average hourly rate for those employees
3. Estimate hours saved per week per employee (conservative: 1-2 hrs, moderate: 3-5 hrs, aggressive: 6+ hrs)
4. Click "Calculate ROI" to see annual productivity value and ROI multiple
5. Use results to justify training budget and set success metrics

---

### 3. Microlearning Modules

**WHO:** Busy professionals who need flexible, bite-sized learning  
**PURPOSE:** Master the THINK Framework in 5-10 minute sessions you can complete during coffee breaks  
**TIME:** 5-10 minutes per module (7 modules total = 35-70 minutes)  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Start with Module 1 (Introduction to THINK Framework)
2. Complete one module per day or at your own pace
3. Apply each concept immediately in your daily work
4. Review modules as needed for refreshers

---

### 4. Role-Based Learning Paths

**WHO:** Professionals who want training tailored to their specific job function  
**PURPOSE:** Learn AI literacy skills relevant to your role (Marketing, HR, Operations, etc.)  
**TIME:** 2-4 hours per path  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Select the path that matches your role
2. Follow the customized curriculum with role-specific examples
3. Complete the real-world applications exercises
4. Apply learned skills to your actual work projects

---

### 5. Certification Program

**WHO:** Professionals who want recognized credentials to demonstrate AI proficiency  
**PURPOSE:** Earn stackable certificates that enhance your resume and LinkedIn profile  
**TIME:** Foundation (2-3 weeks), Practitioner (4-6 weeks), Master (8-12 weeks)  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Review requirements for each certification level
2. Complete the required modules, assessments, and tools
3. Submit completion evidence (self-paced, no exams)
4. Receive digital certificate and LinkedIn badge
5. Progress through levels as your skills advance

---

### 6. Quick Start Guide

**WHO:** Managers implementing AI literacy training for their team  
**PURPOSE:** Launch AI literacy training in just 3 days with a proven framework  
**TIME:** 3 days (Day 1: Setup, Day 2: Training, Day 3: Practice)  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Download the PDF guide
2. Follow the day-by-day implementation plan
3. Use provided talking points and activities
4. Monitor team progress using the readiness checklist

---

### 7. THINK Framework

**WHO:** Anyone using AI tools in their work  
**PURPOSE:** Apply a systematic approach to AI integration that maintains critical thinking  
**TIME:** 15-20 minutes to learn, ongoing application  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Learn each letter: Target, Harness, Inquire, Navigate, Keep
2. Use the Manager Planning Tool before assigning AI-assisted work
3. Apply the framework to every AI interaction
4. Reference quick cards for daily reminders

---

### 8. Building Independence (Ownership Verification)

**WHO:** Managers assessing employee AI literacy, employees self-checking understanding  
**PURPOSE:** Ensure employees maintain ownership of their work when using AI  
**TIME:** 5 minutes per check  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Review the 7 ownership indicators
2. Check boxes for demonstrated skills
3. Identify gaps where additional support is needed
4. Use as a coaching tool, not a grading system

---

### 9. Implementation Guides (Training Session Plans)

**WHO:** Managers, trainers, L&D professionals delivering AI literacy training  
**PURPOSE:** Ready-to-use training session plans with scripts and activities  
**TIME:** 45-60 minutes per session  
**HOW TO USE:**
1. Select the training session topic
2. Review the session plan and materials
3. Deliver training using provided scripts and activities
4. Customize examples for your industry/team

### 10. AI Ethics

**WHO:** All employees, especially those in decision-making roles  
**PURPOSE:** Understand and apply ethical AI principles in your work  
**TIME:** 30-45 minutes  
**HOW TO USE:**

4. Discuss ethical dilemmas with your team

**TIME:** 10-20 minutes per activity

**WHO:** Individual employees tracking their AI learning journey

## Implementation Notes

- Place instruction boxes at the TOP of each section (before main content)
- Use consistent formatting across all sections
- Keep language professional but friendly
- Focus on practical value, not features
- Include time estimates to set expectations
- Emphasize "no grading" for self-assessment tools
