About Prompted

Building the future of AI literacy education

Our Mission

The Prompted: AI Mastery Platform is a comprehensive K-12 AI literacy solution designed to move students beyond basic competence to strategic mastery. We provide a complete 8-week implementation system with curriculum, assessments, teacher resources, and administrative guidance.

Developed by a technology education specialist in close collaboration with experienced classroom educators, every resource is grounded in real classroom needs and pedagogical best practices.

Our Approach

We believe students should become AI masters, not AI dependents. Our platform emphasizes:

Critical Thinking: Students learn to evaluate AI output, identify errors, and verify information systematically.

Independence: AI enhances learning but never replaces the essential work of developing skills and understanding.

Strategic Use: Students make informed decisions about when, how, and why to use AI based on learning objectives.

Ethical Practice: The THINK Framework ensures students consider truthfulness, helpfulness, inspiration, necessity, and kindness in every AI interaction.

"We're not teaching students to use AI. We're teaching them to think WITH AI while maintaining their intellectual independence, creativity, and confidence. The students who master this balance won't just succeed academically‚Äîthey'll thrive in a future where human judgment, creativity, and critical thinking remain irreplaceable."
