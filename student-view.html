Interactive Classroom Activities

üé≠ AI Debate Protocol

Time: 50 minutes

Setup:

  • Group A: Argues position using AI assistance
  • Group B: Argues position without AI
  • Group C: Judges effectiveness

Process:

  1. Research phase (15 min)
  2. Argument development (10 min)
  3. Debate (15 min)
  4. Judgment and discussion (10 min)

Debrief Questions:

  • Which arguments were stronger? Why?
  • How did AI help or hinder?
  • What did each group learn?

üîç AI Error Hunt

Challenge: Find mistakes in AI responses

Categories:

  • Historical facts
  • Scientific explanations
  • Mathematical solutions
  • Literary analysis
  • Current events

Scoring System:

  • 1 point: Identify error
  • 2 points: Explain why it's wrong
  • 3 points: Provide correct info with source

Evaluation: Which prompts produced most effective responses for each audience?
